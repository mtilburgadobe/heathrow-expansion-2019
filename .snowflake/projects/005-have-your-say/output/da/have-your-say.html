The consultation
case.title
Your views will shape the final application
case.prose

This is our statutory consultation on our preferred proposals for a third runway at Heathrow. The consultation runs from 18 June until 11:55pm on 13 September 2019. Feedback received after the deadline may not be taken into consideration.

You can respond in four ways — online using our feedback form, by email, by post using a freepost address, or by phone. You can also visit one of 50+ consultation events across London and the South East to speak to the Heathrow team in person, view a model of the expanded airport, and watch fly-through videos of the proposals.

Four topic areas

Please use the feedback questionnaire to respond to our consultation. The 24 questions are organised across four topic areas. You can answer all questions or just those that matter most to you.
card-1.num
Q1–5 — Masterplan
card-1.title
The Preferred Masterplan
card-1.body
Questions on specific masterplan components, proposed site relocations, boundary design proposals, noise management around the perimeter, and any other aspects of the masterplan.
card-2.num
Q6–11 — Construction & Operations
card-2.title
Building and operating the airport
card-2.body
Questions on runway alternation timing, night flight proposals, directional preference, early growth, surface access commitments, and other future operations topics.
card-3.num
Q12–21 — Environment & Community
card-3.title
Responsible development
card-3.body
Questions on managing environmental effects, environmentally managed growth limits, the Community Fund, property compensation schemes, and other community impact topics.
card-4.num
Q22–24 — Your Views
card-4.title
Any other comments
card-4.body
Three final questions covering any other aspects of the proposals not addressed elsewhere, plus any information you want us to take into account and your preferred option overall.
